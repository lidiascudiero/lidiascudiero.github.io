About Me Treating data not just as numbers, but as the behavior of a complex system

Get to know me!

I am a Data Scientist with my background in Cognitive Neuroscience and Big Data Analytics. My approach is rooted in the study of complex systems: I treat data as a dynamic behavioral signal, decoding hidden patterns rather than just processing rows of information.

With a rigorous experimental mindset, I build end-to-end ML pipelines—from raw data preprocessing to fine-tuned and explainable modeling (XAI) and interactive visualization. My hands-on expertise includes Predictive Maintenance for IoT, Time-Series Analysis, and advanced NLP techniques like Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) and Sentiment Analysis.

I excel at bridging the gap between technical teams and stakeholders by translating complex analytical results into interactive dashboards and evidence-based reports. I am driven to apply data-centric solutions across healthtech, intelligent systems, and behavioral analytics.
